  1. Sushi/Sashimi すし/さしみ

    2. (Sushi)

    » Sushi is a traditional Japanese food made with vinegared rice, typically seasoned with sugar and salt and combined with a variety of ingredients such as seafood, vegetables or meat.. Seafood is the most comon and may be served either raw, cooked or marinated. While sushi comes in numerous styles and presentations, the defining component is the vinegared rice also known as shari (シャリ).

    Different Types of Sushi
    • chirashizishi(ちらし寿司)
    • Makizushi(巻き寿司)
    • Inarizushi(いなり寿司)
    • Narezushi(馴れ寿司)
    • Nigirizushi(握り寿司)
    • Oshizushi(押し寿司)
